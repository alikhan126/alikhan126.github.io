About

I'm a backend engineer in New York City with 8+ years building systems that stay up when the traffic is real and the failure modes are expensive. Most of my work lives in the unglamorous parts: queues that don't lose work, databases that don't fall over, identity and signing flows that have to be correct every single time.

For the last four years I've been on the founding team at Signit, where I took the backend from a prototype to a platform serving 700+ enterprise customers at 2M+ daily API requests. Along the way I chased down a Redis memory crisis hiding 753,765 orphaned tasks, reclaimed 89GB from a production database, and built a GenAI document platform on LangGraph and Qdrant. I like the problems that only show up at scale.

Next I'm joining Veris AI as a founding engineer, building simulation environments and post-training pipelines for enterprise AI agents. I recently finished an MS in Data Science at Pace University.
